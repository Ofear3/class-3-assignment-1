About PCIST


The Programming Club of IST (pcIST) is dedicated to fostering a community of passionate programmers. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to enhance their coding skills, collaborate on projects, and stay updated with the latest industry trends.

We organize regular workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures from industry experts to help our members grow both technically and professionally. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, pcIST welcomes you to join us on this exciting journey of learning and innovation.
